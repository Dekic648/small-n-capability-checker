# Small n Capability Checker

Check what your small usability sample can actually detect — before you promise stakeholders something your study can't deliver.

**[Try it live →](https://dekic648.github.io/small-n-capability-checker/)**

## What it does

Most usability studies run with 5–15 participants. That's fine for finding problems, but stakeholders often want statistical claims your sample size can't support. This tool shows you exactly what's detectable at your sample size so you can set expectations upfront.

- Enter your sample size and see detection capabilities
- Shows minimum detectable differences and power analysis
- Helps you frame findings honestly in reports
